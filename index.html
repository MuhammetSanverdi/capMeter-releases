Features

Everything you need to stay in control

For anyone who relies on Claude every day — from casual chats to heavy coding.

📊

Real-time usage ring

A circular progress icon in your menu bar shows your current session usage at a glance. Color shifts green → orange → red as you approach the limit.

🎯

Limit prediction

CapMeter watches your burn rate and tells you when you'll hit your limit — so you can pace yourself before you're locked out.

🧩

Per-model limits

See your session, weekly, and per-model caps (Opus, Fable and more) side by side — pulled live from your Claude account.

🔔

Smart notifications

Get alerted at custom thresholds (80%, 90% by default). Set quiet hours so you're never disturbed while sleeping.

📈

Usage history

14-day charts, weekly comparison and trends. Export your data as CSV or JSON anytime.

👤

Multiple accounts

Switch between Claude accounts instantly. Each account's usage and history is tracked independently.

🪟

Floating widget

A draggable mini-widget that floats above all your windows and spaces — perfect while coding in the terminal.

🔗

Slack & webhook alerts

Send rich Slack messages or HTTP webhooks when thresholds are crossed — you choose the destination.
